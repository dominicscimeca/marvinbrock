On graduation the young Bermudian was hired by the station as a reporter. He was continuously concerned however that he didn’t have the deep radio voice that many at the time had on radio and decided to move into television. He had been exposed to television earlier when co-hosting JD William’s dance program on local station WRAL TV. In the early seventies he was hired by WGHP-TV in High Point North Carolina and worked his way up to Bureau Chief of its Greensboro office.
