Marvin Brock

With new health and vigor Marvin determined it was time to get to work but with no income the first step would be to work for someone. He sought to get employ using the skills and competency he acquired in acupressure and massage over the years. A local doctor offered him a position in his group. Marvin soon discovered however that in Oregon there was no reciprocity with other states. That meant his certification in those area, valid in California could not be used. In order to be recertified in Oregon he discovered that going back to school was the best option. There was little difference in the time required to become recertified and becoming an acupuncturist, decided to complete his studies in Oriental medicine become a doctor of Oriental medicine.

There was a hiccup along the way, to gain admission to either of the two Oriental medical school in the Portland area he had to satisfy the prerequisites. Portland State University accepted Marvin’s undergraduate degree and he began studying anatomy and physiology, psychology, physics and chemistry. He was inspired to apply to the National College of Natural Medicine and was accepted in its doctoral program Oriental medicine. Marvin’s journey continues…
